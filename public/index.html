Pipeline Pigging Schedule

This application will allow your pipeline maintenance team to record and schedule their pigging activities while out in the field. It eliminates the need for cumbersome spreadsheets, and allows Pipeline Operators to enter their pigging activities into the app using their mobile phones.

It also enables the Pipeline Foreman to manage the application. The foreman's administrative functions include adding pipelines, updating the pigging frequencies, and making corrections to the pigging history as well as managing user access to the application.

Additionally, a third class of User (called a Report Viewer), has the ability to view Pigging Schedules and Debris Reports.
